This is Qalaquri

The restaurant "ქალაქური" was founded in 1998 by a passionate Georgian chef named Tamara Bagrationi. Inspired by her grandmother's traditional recipes and the rich culinary heritage of Georgia, Tamara decided to open a cozy eatery in the heart of Kutaisi. The restaurant quickly became popular for its authentic dishes, warm atmosphere, and the love for Georgian culture that Tamara infused into every detail. Over the years, "ქალაქური" has grown to be a beloved local spot, celebrating more than two decades of delicious food and cherished memories.

Fun Facts

Musical Nights: Every Friday evening, the restaurant transforms into a lively music venue where local bands perform traditional Georgian songs, creating a festive atmosphere.

Secret Menu: There’s a hidden menu that only the regulars know about, featuring off-the-menu dishes that the chef prepares using seasonal and local ingredients.

Historical Decor: The walls are adorned with historical photos of Kutaisi, telling the story of the city through captivating images and artifacts.

Cooking Classes: Twice a month, the restaurant offers cooking classes where you can learn to make traditional Georgian dishes, led by the head chef.

Signature Dish: The restaurant is famous for its special khachapuri, a cheese-filled bread that's so popular it's often sold out by the end of the night.

Celebrity Spotting: It's a favorite spot for local celebrities, so don't be surprised if you see a famous face or two dining next to you!

Seasonal Garden: In the warmer months, the restaurant opens its beautiful garden seating area, offering a serene dining experience surrounded by blooming flowers and greenery.
